About Me

      I'm Jhansi Malleti, a Passionate UI/UX Designer with a strong interest in creating smooth and engaging user experiences. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and user-friendly designs.

My Design Journey

My interest in design started during my college days. That’s one of the reasons I chose Computer Science-it gave me a strong base in technology. But over time, I realized I enjoy the creative side of tech more than coding. I was always curious about how things look, how they work, and how they are designed. That curiosity slowly turned into a passion for UI/UX design.

I’ve always been someone who observes the small details-how people use apps, how a product is designed, how something could be improved. These daily observations help me think like a designer.I often ask myself: "What do users really need? How can I make this better?" This habit has improved my observation and problem-solving skills, which are key in the world of design.

I’m constantly working to improve myself as a designer. Every project, every idea, and every design I create helps me grow. My goal is to build a strong career in UI/UX design, where I can turn creative ideas into real solutions that help people. I’m always eager to learn more about design and explore new ways to make digital experiences better.
